    • reKreate and monTree app

    reKreate and monTree were longest, most time consuming and hardest project in this semester, but at the same time, a project where we learned the most. From the business aspect of creating the company to creating the app. We decided to go with branded visual identity, so we created one visual identity for our digital agency, reKreate, and one for our app, monTree.

    reKreate

    reKreate is a digital agency that is focused on creating apps and digital solutions to help create environmentally friendly surrounding and make our life as easy as possible.

    monTree

    monTree, the first product from the reKreate creative factory, an app that helps you have an overview of your expenses and receipts. At the same time, the app is environmentally friendly because it stops printing receipts.

    We also created a small walk-through our app to showcase how it works.
